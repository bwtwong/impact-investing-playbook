# Impact Investing Playbook
## A Living Framework Repository

From the analyst's first impact thesis to a fund manager's portfolio construction — the frameworks that structure how capital is deployed, measured, and managed for both financial and impact return.

**Live site:** `https://bwtwong.github.io/impact-investing-playbook` *(update once deployed)*

Part of a series alongside [The Consulting Playbook](https://bwtwong.github.io/consulting-playbook), the [Health Commercialisation & Innovation Playbook](https://bwtwong.github.io/health-innovation-playbook), and the [Implementation Science & MEL Cheatsheet](https://bwtwong.github.io/implementation-science-cheatsheet). See the full series at the [notebook hub](https://bwtwong.github.io/notebooks).

## What this is

Covers both **practitioner-level** concepts (impact thesis, measurement, ESG vs impact) and **fund/investor-level** mechanics (deal structuring, portfolio construction, due diligence) — the full picture from analyst to allocator.

### Foundations (4)
- ESG vs Impact Investing
- Developing an Impact Thesis
- Theory of Change (Investor Lens)
- Additionality

### Measurement (3)
- Five Dimensions of Impact (IMP)
- IRIS+
- Impact Risk Assessment

### Deal Structuring (3)
- Blended Finance
- Social Impact Bonds
- Capital Stack & Instrument Selection

### Portfolio & Fund (2)
- Impact Portfolio Construction
- Operating Principles for Impact Management (OPIM)

### Due Diligence (2)
- Impact Due Diligence Process
- Impact-Washing Red Flags

Every framework includes explanation, application steps, pitfalls, and a **practice scenario with AI coaching feedback**.

---

## Key sources

Frameworks are drawn from the Impact Management Project / Impact Frontiers (Five Dimensions of Impact), the Global Impact Investing Network (IRIS+), and the International Finance Corporation (Operating Principles for Impact Management) — the three most widely referenced standards in the field.
